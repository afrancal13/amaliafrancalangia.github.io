The Artist

About

Photo

With a voice that commands both the concert hall and the sanctuary, Amalia brings a rare combination of technical mastery and profound emotional depth to every performance. Her artistry spans the grand operatic repertoire as well as the sacred traditions of Jewish liturgical music.

Currently serving as a featured soloist at Sinai Temple, Amalia brings her voice to audiences in Los Angeles and beyond — from intimate recitals to full orchestral productions.

Her performances are defined by an unwavering commitment to the music, a deep sense of theater, and a voice of exceptional range and luminosity.
